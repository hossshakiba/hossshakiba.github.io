Research

I'm interested in computer vision and deep learning. My long-term goal is to build multimodal, efficient, and responsible AI to better recognize, understand, and interact with our dynamic world, mainly using less data-hungry methods. I am also interested in contributing to GenAI, especially for creative art, visual editing, and restoration.
